CMAS - ShrinkPin™

Cold Chain Monitoring As-A Service with ShrinkPin™ is used for the management of all components of shrinkage found in retail and wholesale operations. In Food & Grocery, perishable shrinkage loss factors significantly contribute to direct erosion of gross margins. ShrinkPin is a software widget that can support NFC, UHF Gen 2 RFID and Temp Sensitive Rfid Tags. The ShrinkPin application uses AI based modulation to determine the most likely sources of shrink based on store-wide patterns of shrinkage.
